About ClearPath Mentoring

CLEARPATH MENTORING is involved in developing analytical ability and math problem solving skills among students across the world. With an experience of more than 11 years, it firmly believes that problem solving skill is not just useful for math and related subjects but in any career one pursues. It is also involved in bringing clarity on what career direction to pursue with confidence. This leads to growth and satisfaction in the chosen career and hence results in happiness and prosperity in life.

About the Founder

Sunil Pant

After a career in Industrial Power Projects Marketing for over two decades, Sunil Pant shifted to Online Math Teaching to pursue his passion and then into Career Counselling.

He has over eleven years of experience in teaching and mentoring students across various educational boards and international locations. He has successfully developed problem-solving skills and analytical ability among hundreds of students based in different countries across the world to make them better equipped to face school exams as well as international math competitions. He also conducts IIT foundation classes.

He is an alumnus of the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee, from where he got a degree in Electrical Engineering. He is also an MBA.
